Welcome to my page!

Richard has been a musician since the early age of 9 Years old. He started playing drums at All Saints Church. Although Drums was the first instrument he learned to play he also wanted to learn to play the Guitar.
After high school he joined a few bands playing various instruments from Guitar to Drums and even some Bass Guitar. He tried to build his craft and passion for music.
He found himself playing and spending alot more time on learning the instrument and got his First guitar at 13 Years old as a birthday present!
Going into the start of Highschool at Maritzburg College in 2007, he started taking Guitar lessons, and entered all the musicals he could being so passionate about music.
In 2012 he Auditions for Idols South Africa, and unfortunately didnt make it through the elimination stages. But returned two years later and got through two of the staging phases
