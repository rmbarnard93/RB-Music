A little bit About Me

Richard has been a musician since the early age of 9 Years old. He started playing drums at All Saints Church. Although Drums was the first instrument he learned to play he also wanted to learn to play the Guitar. Going into the start of Highschool at Maritzburg College in 2007, he started taking Guitar lessons, and entered all the musicals he could being so passionate about music. In 2012 he Auditions for Idols South Africa, and unfortunately didnt make it through the elimination stages. But returned two years later and got through two of the staging phases

During the course of his Musical career, he joined various Bands from highschool and after. His First Band once he got into high school was "Next In Line". They made alot of memorable experiences, and eventually released their own Single, called "Shout Shout". After school, Richard joined another band called Offshore. They got the opportunity to open for Prime Circle, and made their own original music, Richard was the drummer for this band. At the same time he was part of a Hard Rock Blues band, called "The Budding Hippy's" where Richard played Drums. They performed plenty of shows in the Kwazulu-Natal region.
